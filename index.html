Characters

Awkward Cow

The socially inept protagonist

Awkward cow is on a mission to retrieve his milk back from the Raccoon Den. He has the largest hitbox and jump power among the playable characters.

Blubasaur

The adventurous cave-dweller

Blubasaur is the first friend Awkward Cow encounters throughout his journey. Based off a completely original design, Blubasaur is the smallest and fastest character.

Amogus

The sussy gun-slinger

Another original character, Amogus is the cold-blooded killer of anything with a pulse. He is the only character capable of attacking, however he does not know how to jump.
